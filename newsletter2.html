NOVEMBER IS HERE!

This month's roundup of DJ mixes covers even more ground than usual-from a West African survey put together by Berlin's No Frills crew, to Andrew Peklers attempt to reimagine exotica via avant-garde electronic.

It manages some serious time-travelling, too-all the way back to one of Derrick May's earth-shaking sets from Detroit's Music Institute, circa 1988. Grab your headphones and some comfortable shoes, and prepare to roam.

For more music, including sets from Prins Thomas, Via App, and Yves Tumor, be sure to check last month's column.
